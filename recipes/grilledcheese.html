Bomb Ass Grilled Cheese

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt on sliced tomatoes and let drain. This helps it stay a little drier so the tomatoes don't leak all over the bread.
  2. Place a slice of cheddar, tomato, basil on each slice of bread and sprinkle with gruyere
  3. To Cook: Turn sandwich over and fry on the other side. Both sides should be equally browned and the cheese melted just enough to squeeze out the sides of the sandwich.
