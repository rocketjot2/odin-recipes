Ina Garten's Penne Alla Vodka

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Yellow Onions
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • Dried Oregano
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Vodka
  • 2 Cans of Peeled Plum Tomatoes
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Penne

Steps:

  1. Preheat to 375 degrees
  2. Simmer onions in Dutch Oven until translucent
  3. Cook Pasta
  4. Pour Tomato Mixture into blender
  5. Add to Pasta
